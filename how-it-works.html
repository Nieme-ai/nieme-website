HOW IT WORKS

Every operation follows the same lifecycle.

Operation: "Prepare the quarterly board report."
START A person delegates the operation
RUNTIME Runtime assembles the right context
VAULT Vault supplies your knowledge, standards, history
MODELS Models reason and draft
ARTIFACT Artifact produced: the board report
REVIEW A person approves
LEDGER Ledger records what happened
MEMORY Institutional memory grows

A person decides. AI prepares and executes. The organization learns. Every operation in Nieme follows this lifecycle, which is what makes the whole system an operating system for delegated work.

The novel core
EVERY OPERATION, ON THE RECORD

Anyone can claim their AI remembers. Nieme proves what happened.

Every operation is recorded: the evidence it used, the work it produced, who approved it, when. The record is permanent, it can't be quietly rewritten, and it belongs to you. It isn't an audit feature bolted on afterward. It's the spine of the system.

Every operation has a permanent track record.
Every output traceable to its evidence.
Every decision auditable.

No other AI system gives your organization a permanent, owned record of every operation. This is what makes intelligence compound, not just execute.

DELEGATE WITHOUT LOSING CONTROL

Nothing meaningful ships without a person.

You decide what Nieme can do on its own and what needs your sign-off. Reversible, internal work can run freely. Anything that leaves the building, sent, published, paid or deleted, waits for a human. Every action is scoped, recorded, and reversible-by-design where it can be.

Policies you set.
Approvals where they matter.
Delegation you control.
A record you own.
YOUR ORGANIZATION'S KNOWLEDGE, KEPT

Your organization should never lose what it learns.

The Vault holds your organization's knowledge, context, standards, history, as a living body that grows with every operation. Rented AI starts from zero every morning. Nieme carries what you've learned forward. Good decisions become reusable. Hard lessons become future guidance. It compounds, and it stays yours.

CONTEXT BEFORE INTELLIGENCE

Nieme decides what the models should know.

Before any AI begins working, the Runtime works out what the request actually is, what knowledge is relevant, what policies apply, and what past work matters. Only then is the right context assembled and handed to a model. Your whole organization never gets shipped wholesale to a model. Models reason. The Runtime decides what they should see.

HOW THE SYSTEM COMPOUNDS

Every operation is an experiment.

Organizations improve the same way science advances, not through intuition alone, but through a continuous cycle of hypothesis, execution, observation, evidence, and iteration.

Most organizations are terrible at remembering their experiments. Knowledge lives in people's heads and walks out the door when they leave. Decisions get made on folklore instead of evidence.

Nieme automates that cycle and keeps the record.

  • Operations run the experiments.
  • The Ledger records them.
  • The Vault preserves what was learned.
  • The Runtime applies that learning to the next operation.

Every operation becomes an experiment. Every result becomes evidence. Every piece of evidence strengthens institutional knowledge. Every future decision starts from a better foundation.

BUILT LIKE AN OPERATING SYSTEM

Nieme separates the parts most AI products fuse together.

The kernel

Runtime

Schedules operations, composes context, applies policy. It decides what each model should see.

The processor

Models

Interchangeable reasoning. Swap them without losing your context, your record, or anything that matters.

The filesystem

Vault

Your institutional knowledge. Context, standards, and history that compound with every operation.

The journal

Ledger

The permanent operational record. Every operation, who approved it, what it produced. Owned by you, tamper-evident by design.

The interface

Aion

How you work with the system. Delegate operations, review artifacts, and track what's happened.

The processes

Operations

The unit of delegated work. Every task your team gives Nieme runs as a governed, recorded operation.

Swap the model; keep everything that makes it yours.
