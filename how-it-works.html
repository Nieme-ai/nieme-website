YOUR ORGANIZATION'S KNOWLEDGE, KEPT

CONTEXT BEFORE INTELLIGENCE

Nieme decides what the models should know.

Before any AI begins working, the Runtime works out what the request actually is, what knowledge is relevant, what policies apply, and what past work matters. Only then is the right context assembled and handed to a model. Your whole organization never gets shipped wholesale to a model. Models reason. The Runtime decides what they should see.

HOW THE SYSTEM COMPOUNDS

Every operation leaves proof.

Organizations improve the way science does, through evidence rather than intuition. Most organizations are terrible at keeping theirs. Knowledge lives in people's heads and walks out the door when they leave. Decisions get made on folklore. Nieme records every operation, keeps the evidence, and applies it to the next one.

Operations do the work.
The Ledger records them.
The Vault preserves what was learned.
The Runtime applies that learning to the next operation.

Every operation leaves a result. Every result becomes evidence. Every piece of evidence strengthens institutional knowledge. Every future decision starts from a better foundation.

Decision quality emerges from evidence. Evidence emerges from operations. Nieme keeps every one.
